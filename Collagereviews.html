Anonymous = "Summit Valley University has exceeded all my expectations as a Computer Science major. The faculty members are not just educators but mentors who actively engage in our learning journeys. I remember Professor Malik personally helping me debug a project after hours—it shows their commitment to students. The campus fosters creativity with its collaborative study spaces and tech-enabled classrooms. The annual Innovation Week is a highlight; last year, I got to pitch my app idea to actual investors! Although the cafeteria food can be hit-or-miss, everything else more than makes up for it. This place doesn’t just prepare you for a career—it shapes you as a thinker."*
